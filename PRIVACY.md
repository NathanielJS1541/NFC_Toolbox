# Privacy Policy for NFC Toolbox

**Effective Date:** March 8, 2026

## Overview

NFC Toolbox ("the App") is a cross-platform application that provides a suite of tools for working with various NFC tags. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

## Information Collection and Use

**NFC Toolbox does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from its users.**

The App operates entirely on your device and does not:
- Collect personal data such as name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information
- Track user behavior or usage patterns
- Transmit data to external servers or third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Access contacts, location, or other personal data stored on your device (beyond what is necessary for NFC functionality)

## NFC Functionality

The App requires access to your device's NFC hardware to read and write NFC tags. All NFC operations are performed locally on your device. No data read from or written to NFC tags is collected, stored, or transmitted by the App beyond your device.

## Data Storage

Any data you interact with while using the App (such as NFC tag contents) is processed locally on your device. The App does not maintain any databases or storage systems that collect or retain your information.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect or process your personal information.

## Children's Privacy

Since the App does not collect any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
